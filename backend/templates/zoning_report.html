Methodology

This report aggregates publicly available government data from multiple sources. Property assessments are triennial and may not reflect current market values. Demographics are estimated from ACS 5-year survey data (2019–2023 collection period). Crime statistics reflect a 90-day rolling window with an approximately 7-day reporting lag. Median values are interpolated from bracket distributions where pre-computed figures are unavailable. Bulk zoning standards are retrieved via semantic search over the indexed Chicago Municipal Code and may not capture recent amendments.
